Orson Scott Card

Orson is an American author. Born in 1951. His love for writing began at the age of 10. He wrote a short about bullies which eventually led to his best known novel, Ender's Game. Orson is married to Kristine Allen and had five children. His best known is Ender's Game. It's about a boy who is has gifted mind and is recruited by the military to help fight a war.
