Hans Zimmer

Hans Zimmer is a German Film Score composer and music producer. He was born in Frankfurt, Germany on September 12, 1957. He began his career early by playing the piano. Not being a fan of traditional teachings, he started to learn by himself. He admired Ennio Morricone and wanted to become a film composer.
